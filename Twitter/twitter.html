Twitter

Welcome to our #Twitter Vader Anslysis Review page!

We utilized Twitter API and the Vader Sentiment Analysis to see just how people felt about each of the top 10 restaurants. We quickly found a limitation to this by discovering that tweets older than 1 week from the time of searching are not included in the results.

To account for this, we decided that restaurants that received ANY tweets about it in a week span, and therefore contributed to a vader analysis received 0.5 points on the NicKris scale while restaurants that received ANY tweets about it in a week span AND had a higher sentiment score than negative score received a full point.

Results

The charts above show the Vader Sentiment Analysis for five of the ten restaurants in our "Top Ten" list.
